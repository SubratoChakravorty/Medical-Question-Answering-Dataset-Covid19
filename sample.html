My husband works everywhere including a short trip to east London last week. He's showing symptoms of the covid 19 virus. What should I do as the flu medication is not helping. ?

1 doctor weighed in
Dr. Jan frederik Kirsten

In brief: Flu

Self isolation. Make sure he has not been in contact with anyone with Covid-19.

Get him tested as soon as possible.
Relax. Most of these symptoms are that of the normal flu.
Hope he feel better soon.
